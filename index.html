Grego Gallery

Portrait & Fine Art Photography based in Cincinnati, Ohio.

Gregory Thomas Engler, Jr. is an American photographer born in Ohio & currently attending Miami University in Oxford. Part time Gregory works as a Lab Technician and Student Monitor at MU’s College of Creative Arts in their traditional Darkroom & Film Development lab. This space is also home to several large format digital printers, and a fully outfitted photographic lighting studio.

Gregory majors in Marketing at Miami University’s Farmer School of Business and works simultaneously towards building a classical foundation for his photographic endeavors through the completion of a Minor in Photography.

Thus far, Gregory has had the opportunity to gain position’s contributing to several on campus publications including MUF&D and UP Magazine, and hopes to broaden the scope of his work even further in his last few university semesters.
